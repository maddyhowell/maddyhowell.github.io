Hi, I am a third year PhD student at Monash University in Melbourne, Australia. I study the asteroseismic signals of red giants in old metal-poor globular clusters. Asteroseismology is the science of detecting oscillations in the interiors of stars, and can be used to determine a wealth of knowledge about the individual star.
